Online and Offline Fighting Games

Frawlsalla is a famous platform based fighting game. In Frawlsalla you can play with your friends online with internet connection and ofline via lan connection to fighting together. Frawsalla is a free to play fighting game that support cross platform from pc, console, Xbox, and mobile so you can play whenever you want.

Many Modes to Choose From

You can enjoy playing Frawlsalla with various mode of like Casual Fighting 1 vs 1 battle, team battle that you be able to team up with your friends, Free for All Battles, Local Single Player, Local custom room, Practice room, and Ranked Match to make you at the top of Ranked Leaderboard.
